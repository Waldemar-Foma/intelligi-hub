The website is designed to support the existing intellectual base and/or acquire new knowledge for students in grades 5 - 11.

ABOUT THE PROJECT

The project is designed for extracurricular educational activities for students in grades 5-11. The website will be useful for students who have forgotten something or for those who did not know but are interested in the topic.

Website features

Simplified interface

The website interface allows even those unfamiliar with gadgets to navigate it easily.

Stability

Updates that enhance the stability of the website's performance.

Reliability

Thanks to the website's lack of unnecessary information overload, minimal device resources are utilized, ensuring stable operation.

Information accessibility

All theory and practice are sorted into tabs for greater convenience.
